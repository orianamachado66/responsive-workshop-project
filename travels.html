Mallorca Island - Spain

Mallorca, Spain, is a stunning Mediterranean gem that promises an unforgettable escape. Immerse yourself in crystal-clear waters, explore picturesque villages, and savor delectable Spanish cuisine. From charming local markets to historic castles, Mallorca offers a diverse and authentic island experience.

Puebla - Mexico

Puebla, Mexico, is a delightful blend of rich traditions and mouthwatering dishes. Experience the famous mole poblano, wander through historic streets, and join lively celebrations. Explore local crafts like Talavera pottery, savor authentic mariachi music, and let Puebla's charm captivate you.
